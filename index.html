About the output & safety notes
• Fanuc G76 (P m r a / Q min / R finish, then X Z R P Q F) is the most common lathe threading cycle.
• G92 / G33 list every pass explicitly — easy to verify and tune by hand.
• All depths shown are radial. Minor Ø = Major Ø − 2 × depth.
• Verify spindle direction: RH = M03, LH = M04 (helix follows feed direction).
• Confirm tool nose angle, stock turned to major Ø, and that lead-in/out clears the part before running.
• Always dry-run / single-block the first part. Constants suit typical stock; adjust for your material & insert.
